## Risk Management

### Top Risks & Mitigation

**Risk 1: Team Resistance**
- **Likelihood:** Medium
- **Impact:** High
- **Mitigation:**
  - Clear communication of WHY
  - Involve team in process design
  - Celebrate early wins
  - Address concerns in 1:1s

**Risk 2: Client Dissatisfaction**
- **Likelihood:** Medium
- **Impact:** Very High
- **Mitigation:**
  - Proactive communication
  - Manage expectations early
  - Show tangible improvements quickly
  - Over-deliver on key milestones

**Risk 3: Productivity Drop**
- **Likelihood:** High
- **Impact:** Medium
- **Mitigation:**
  - Plan for 20% capacity reduction
  - Extend timelines proactively
  - Focus on quality, not quantity
  - Temporary contractors for critical work

**Risk 4: Senior Developer Burnout**
- **Likelihood:** Medium
- **Impact:** High
- **Mitigation:**
  - Limit to 15 hours/week commitment
  - Rotate review duties
  - Provide support and recognition
  - Hire additional senior if needed

**Risk 5: Poor Adoption**
- **Likelihood:** Low
- **Impact:** Very High
- **Mitigation:**
  - Automated enforcement (ArchUnit)
  - CI/CD blocks poor code
  - Regular assessments
  - Clear consequences for non-compliance
